Consecrate | Good Friday Service

April 3, 2026 from 6:00 - 7:00pm MST

Join us for a special Good Friday gathering called Consecrate as we step onto our church property together for the first time to worship and pray. On this sacred evening, we will consecrate the land to the Lord, reflecting on the sacrifice of Jesus through worship, Scripture, and communion. As we remember the passion of Christ, we will also look forward with faith to the future God is preparing for our church and our city. This will be a simple but meaningful hour of worship and prayer as we dedicate this place and ourselves to God’s purposes. Come ready to reflect, worship, and seek the presence of God together.
